About Us

Wunderchat is a mobile chat platform that provides a seamless, enriching and delightful chat experience to its users. Relevant in both informal and business contexts, Wunderchat ’s features are carefully designed to be both engaging and efficient.
Apart from being a chat product Wunderchat also has features for online payment and integrations with other apps that facilitate a “one-stop-app” experience.
